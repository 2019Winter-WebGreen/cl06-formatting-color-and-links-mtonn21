Golden Retrievers; The Best Dogs!

All about golden retrievers and why they make the best pets!

The Golden Retriever is is a large-sized breed of dog. They are dark golden, cream, light golden, and or golden.

They were orginally bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl, such as ducks and upland game birds, during hunting and shooting parties, and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged. Golden retrievers are known to be kind, intelliget, reliable, trustworthy, confident and friendy. These dogs make for a perfect friend that you can play with. They love to run around and be adventurous. Golden retrievers would never bight you. They are the most friendliest dogs you will ever meet. Golden retrievers form close relationships with thier owners and will rarley run away.

If you are intrested in buying a dog, you should definitley look into golden retrivers. There are so many different places you can buy them from. Including, Petsmart and the ASPCA.
